### 1. ENGAGEMENT

The Company engages the Contractor as an independent contractor to provide transportation services using vehicles rented from the Company or approved by the Company. The Contractor accepts this engagement and agrees to perform services in accordance with this Agreement.

### 2. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

The Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or partner of the Company. The Contractor:

- Has sole discretion over their work schedule, routes, and methods
- Is free to provide services to other companies
- Bears all expenses related to their business operations
- Is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and benefits
- Is not entitled to employee benefits, workers' compensation, or unemployment insurance
- Has no authority to bind the Company to any agreement

### 3. SERVICES

The Contractor agrees to provide the following services:

- Operation of luxury SUV vehicles for passenger transportation
- Compliance with all TNC (Transportation Network Company) requirements, including Uber Black and Lyft Black standards
- Provision of professional, courteous service to all passengers
- Maintenance of vehicle cleanliness and presentation standards
- Timely communication with the Company regarding vehicle condition and availability

### 4. VEHICLE RENTAL

The Contractor may rent vehicles from the Company under separate Vehicle Rental Agreements. The terms of each rental, including fees, mileage, and duration, are governed by those separate agreements. This Agreement does not guarantee the availability of any specific vehicle.

### 5. COMPENSATION

The Contractor's compensation structure is as follows:

- **Retained Earnings:** The Contractor retains all fares, tips, and gratuities received from passengers through TNC platforms or private clients
- **Rental Fees:** The Contractor pays the Company the agreed rental fees as specified in each Vehicle Rental Agreement
- **No Wage or Salary:** The Company does not pay wages, salaries, hourly rates, or commissions to the Contractor
- **No Minimum Guarantee:** The Company does not guarantee any minimum level of earnings or business volume

### 6. TNC PLATFORMS

The Contractor acknowledges that:

- They are solely responsible for maintaining their own accounts with Uber, Lyft, and any other TNC platforms
- They must comply with all TNC platform terms, conditions, and vehicle requirements
- The Company does not control TNC platform algorithms, dispatch, or pricing
- The Contractor bears all risk of deactivation, suspension, or reduced demand on TNC platforms
- Any disputes with TNC platforms are solely between the Contractor and the platform

### 7. PRIVATE CLIENTS

The Contractor may build and maintain their own private clientele. The Company may provide leads, referrals, and marketing support at its discretion. Private client relationships belong to the Contractor, subject to any non-solicitation terms in this Agreement.

### 8. INSURANCE

The Contractor acknowledges that:

- The Company provides commercial auto insurance for Company-owned vehicles during rental periods
- The Contractor is responsible for the deductible amount per incident
- The Contractor should maintain their own personal health, disability, and liability insurance
- The Contractor is not covered by the Company's workers' compensation insurance

### 9. TAXES

The Contractor is solely responsible for:

- Reporting all income on their tax returns
- Paying all applicable federal, state, and local taxes
- Making estimated tax payments as required by law
- Obtaining any necessary business licenses or permits

### 10. STANDARDS AND COMPLIANCE

The Contractor agrees to:

- Maintain a professional appearance and demeanor at all times
- Keep vehicles clean, well-maintained, and properly branded
- Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and TNC requirements
- Not operate vehicles under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Maintain a valid driver's license and clean driving record
- Not engage in discriminatory conduct

### 11. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Contractor agrees not to disclose or use any confidential information of the Company, including but not limited to:
- Customer lists and contact information
- Pricing strategies and operational methods
- Proprietary business processes and systems
- Financial information

This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

### 12. NON-SOLICITATION

For a period of ________ months following termination of this Agreement, the Contractor agrees not to:
- Solicit or divert any of the Company's rental customers or drivers
- Hire or engage any of the Company's contractors or employees
- Use the Company's branding, trademarks, or trade names

### 13. INDEMNIFICATION

The Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Company, its members, managers, employees, and agents from any claims, damages, liabilities, and expenses arising from:
- The Contractor's negligence or misconduct
- Violation of any law or regulation
- Breach of this Agreement
- Claims by passengers, third parties, or regulatory authorities

### 14. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon ________ days' written notice. The Company may terminate immediately for:
- Breach of this Agreement
- Conduct that endangers passengers or the public
- Criminal activity
- Deactivation from Uber or Lyft
- Providing false information

### 15. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

**Mediation:** The parties agree to attempt to resolve any dispute through mediation administered by a mutually agreed mediator in Travis County, Texas before pursuing litigation.

**Arbitration:** If mediation is unsuccessful, any dispute shall be resolved by binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association in Travis County, Texas.

**Waiver of Class Action:** The Contractor waives any right to participate in a class action or collective action against the Company.

### 16. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.

### 17. SEVERABILITY

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

### 18. AMENDMENT

This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.
