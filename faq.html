Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with Ezra Mobility Group.

General

What is Ezra Mobility Group?

Ezra Mobility Group is a premium black SUV rental company based in Austin, Texas. We rent luxury SUVs — Chevrolet Suburban, Ford Expedition, Lincoln Navigator, and Cadillac Escalade — to gig economy drivers who want to operate on Uber Black, Lyft Black, or build their own private car service.

Who can rent from you?

Anyone 25 years or older with a valid driver's license, a clean driving record, and a passing background check. You don't need to be an existing rideshare driver — we'll help you get set up.

Do I need my own commercial insurance?

No. Every rental includes commercial insurance coverage. You're covered for Uber Black, Lyft Black, and private rides. You will be responsible for a deductible per incident.

Vehicles

Are the vehicles Uber Black compliant?

Yes — every vehicle in our fleet meets or exceeds Uber Black and Lyft Black eligibility requirements. They are all model year 2023 or newer with premium interiors and all required features.

What vehicles do you have available?

Our fleet includes the Chevrolet Suburban, Ford Expedition, Lincoln Navigator, and Cadillac Escalade. All seat up to 8 passengers and are equipped with premium amenities.

What is the pink bumper guard?

We install pink metal front bumper guards on all our vehicles. It's our signature look that makes you instantly recognizable to clients — especially at airports and pickup locations. It's proven to help clients find their driver faster.

Rental Process

How do I rent a vehicle?

You can submit a booking inquiry through our website, call us at (512) 348-8425, or email info@ezramobilitygroup.com. We'll check availability and get back to you within 24 hours.

What is the application process?

Submit a driver application on our website. We'll review your credentials, run a background check, and verify your driving record. Approved applicants can pick up their vehicle as soon as the same day.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance (personal), and a refundable security deposit. If you're already signed up with Uber or Lyft, bring your TNC account information.

Rental Terms

What are the rental periods?

We offer daily, weekly, and monthly rentals. Daily rentals start at $189/day, weekly at $899/week, and monthly at $2,899/month.

Are there mileage limits?

Yes. Daily rentals include 100 miles, weekly includes 700 miles, and monthly includes 3,000 miles. Overage is charged at $0.30-$0.50 per mile depending on your plan.

Can I take the vehicle out of Texas?

You must have prior written authorization to drive outside of Texas. We generally allow travel to neighboring states for airport runs with notice.

Driving

Can I use the vehicle for Uber X or Lyft?

Our vehicles are specifically for Uber Black and Lyft Black. Using them for standard Uber X or Lyft rides would not be cost-effective. However, you can use them for private car service clients.

Can I build my own private client list?

Absolutely — in fact, we encourage it! We offer a Private Client Leads add-on ($199/month) that provides corporate referrals, airport transfer leads, and marketing support.

What if I get into an accident?

Ensure everyone's safety first, call emergency services if needed, and then contact us immediately at (512) 348-8425. We'll guide you through the process. Our commercial insurance covers the vehicle.

Maintenance & Support

Who handles maintenance?

We handle all scheduled maintenance and unexpected repairs. You just need to report any issues promptly. We provide 24/7 roadside assistance for breakdowns.

What if the vehicle needs repairs?

Contact us immediately. We'll arrange for repairs and provide a replacement vehicle if needed. Do not authorize repairs yourself without our approval.

Can I smoke in the vehicle?

No. All vehicles are strictly non-smoking. A $250 deep cleaning fee will be charged if there is evidence of smoking.

Business

Do you have to form the LLC before renting to me?

No. You can rent as an individual or as a business entity. Many of our drivers start as individuals and form their LLC later as they build their private client base.

Can I hire another driver to operate the vehicle?

No. Vehicles can only be operated by the approved renter. However, you can rent an additional vehicle on our Dual-Vehicle plan to subcontract to another approved driver.

How do I get started?

Apply online or call us at (512) 348-8425. We'll walk you through the process and get you behind the wheel of a premium black SUV.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help. Give us a call or send a message and we'll get back to you within 2 hours.
